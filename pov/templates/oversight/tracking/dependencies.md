## Dependency Health Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Healthy** | Current version, no known issues |
| **Aging** | 1-2 minor versions behind, no critical issues |
| **Stale** | Major version behind or >6 months old |
| **At Risk** | Known vulnerabilities or deprecation |
| **Critical** | Active security issue or breaking in production |
